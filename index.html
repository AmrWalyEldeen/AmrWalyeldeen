About Me

I am an Assistant Lecturer and researcher at the Department of Zoology, Faculty of Science, Cairo University. My research interests span cancer biology, extracellular vesicles, tumor microenvironment, and translational oncology. I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. focusing on proteomic profiling of circulating exosomes from breast cancer patients to identify exosomal proteome signatures as non-invasive, cost-effective biomarkers for early detection and disease monitoring.
